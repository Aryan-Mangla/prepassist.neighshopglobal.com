PrepAssist V2 is a precision tool for Civil Services aspirants. Users are expected to maintain account integrity and prevent unauthorized third-party access to their assigned neural instance.

2. Compute & Credit Matrix

AI credits are the currency of our ecosystem. They are consumed based on the execution weight of specific neural tasks (PDF Parsing, Mains Evaluation, etc.). Unused credits persist based on your tier's archival rules.

Attempting to reverse-engineer our RAG pipeline, scrap our vector datasets, or execute adversarial prompts against our LLM ensemble will result in immediate termination of the auth token.

While our AI provides high-fidelity simulations for UPSC evaluation, PrepAssist is an assistive tool. Final decisions regarding preparation strategies are the sole responsibility of the aspirant.
